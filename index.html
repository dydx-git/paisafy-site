Privacy isn't a setting. It's how it's built.

No account. No sign-up. No server quietly hoarding your spending. Paisafy lives on your phone, for your eyes only.

  • Your accounts, transactions, budgets, bills and goals stay on your device — nowhere else.
  • We collect nothing. We never sell or share your data, and we don't run ads.
  • The AI insights work from summary numbers only — your actual transactions, account names, texts and location stay put.
  • When AI reads a receipt for you, that's processed on your request and never reused — spelled out plainly in our policy.
