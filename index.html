Money, made calm.

Paisafy is a personal finance tracker that keeps your data on your phone. Track expenses, income and transfers across multi-currency accounts; set budgets, goals and bills; and let on-device-friendly AI turn receipts and notifications into clean transactions.

Everything you track, nothing you leak

A full-featured tracker with a quiet design philosophy: money is the loud signal, and your raw data stays put.

🧾

One form for everything

Expense, income or transfer in a single form. Split a purchase across multiple accounts, with per-field smart defaults that remember your common picks.

📊

Budgets & goals

Per-category budgets with weekly/monthly/yearly periods, carry-forward and rollover. Savings, milestone, spend-cut and surplus goals — each in its own currency.

🏦

Accounts & loans

Checking, savings, cash, credit, investment, loan, mortgage, crypto. Net-worth view with assets vs. liabilities, currency-converted to your default.

🔁

Declared bills

You declare recurring bills — never auto-detected. Payments match by title and category, with a full payment history per bill.

✨

Smart, on-device-friendly AI

Parse receipts, statements and payment notifications into transactions. An offline regex fallback means entry keeps working with no network at all.

🌍

True multi-currency

Every amount carries its own currency with currency-safe math. Cross-currency totals convert through live FX rates, with per-currency subtotals offline.

💡

Smart Overview insights

A carousel of insight cards — backward-looking and forward-looking projections — plus an optional AI analyst built from aggregate figures only.

💾

Your data, your backups

Full JSON export/import. Optional daily backups write to a folder you pick — local storage or your own Google Drive — no sign-in required.

Privacy isn't a setting. It's the architecture.

Paisafy is offline-first and local-first by design. There's no account to create and no server collecting your records.

  • Your accounts, transactions, budgets, bills and goals are stored only on your device.
  • We collect nothing. We never sell or share your data, and use none of it for advertising.
  • The AI Overview is built from aggregate figures only — no raw transactions, account names, SMS or location leave the device for it.
  • When you use AI parsing, that specific content is processed by Google as a service provider on your request — disclosed in full in our policy.
